Target Audience

The target audience for the ShowLowCityChamber.com web site is:

  • Who: Residents, Tourists, Businesses, and those who are interested in moving to or setting up businesses in Show Low, Arizona.
  • Age:21 and older.
  • Income: $46,310 to $65,712
  • Motivation: Individuals that want small town living in the mountains.
