Site Name

This site is going to be called ShowLowCityChamber.com. As indicated by the name this site will provide information to attract commerce to Show Low, Arizona.

Site Purpose

The main purpose of ShowLowCityChamber.com is to provide information to potential new residents and businesses that are looking to move to Show Low, Arizona. The objective will be accomplished by providing information about the benefits of living and doing work in Show Low, Arizona.
A secondary purpose for the site is to attact tourist to Show Low and the resorts in Pine Top, Arizona. To accomplish this information about year round attactions will be easily found on the site.
